- \((\mathbb Z,+)\) is a group with identity \(0\) and inverse \(-n\).
- The nonzero rational numbers \(\mathbb Q^\times\) form a group under
  multiplication.
- The symmetries of a square form a finite group under composition.
- The invertible real \(n \times n\) matrices form the general linear group
  \(GL_n(\mathbb R)\).
