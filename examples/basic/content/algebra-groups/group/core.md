A **group** is a [[shared-foundations/set|set]] \(G\) equipped with a
[[shared-foundations/binary-operation|binary operation]] \(\cdot : G \times G
\to G\), an element \(e \in G\), and an inverse operation \(a \mapsto a^{-1}\)
such that the associativity, identity, and inverse axioms hold.

Equivalently, a group is a [[algebra-groups/monoid|monoid]] in which every
element is invertible. The axiomatic view is the canonical core view; folded
views belong in the equivalent-characterizations layer.
