A **vector space** over a [[shared-foundations/field|field]] \(F\) is a
[[shared-foundations/set|set]] \(V\) equipped with vector addition and scalar
multiplication such that vectors form an additive group and scalar multiplication
is compatible with the field operations.

The most compact folded view is: a vector space is an abelian group equipped with
a compatible action of the multiplicative and additive structure of a field.
