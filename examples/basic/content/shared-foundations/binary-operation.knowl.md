A **binary operation** on a [[shared-foundations/set|set]] \(S\) is a
[[shared-foundations/function|function]]
\[
  S \times S \to S.
\]

When the operation is written multiplicatively, the value at \((a,b)\) is often
written \(ab\) or \(a \cdot b\).
