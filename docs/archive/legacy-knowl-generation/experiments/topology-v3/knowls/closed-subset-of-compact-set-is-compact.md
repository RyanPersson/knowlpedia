---
title: "Closed subset of a compact set is compact"
description: "A closed subset of a compact set is compact in the ambient space"
---

**Closed subset of compact is compact:** Let $X$ be a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" section="topology-core" text="topological space" >}}}, let $K\subseteq X$ be {{{< knowl id="compact-set" text="compact" >}}}, and let $F\subseteq X$ be {{{< knowl id="closed-set" section="topology-core" text="closed" >}}} with $F\subseteq K$. Then $F$ is compact.

Equivalently, compactness is inherited by closed subsets (with the {{{< knowl id="subspace-topology" section="topology-core" text="subspace topology" >}}}).
