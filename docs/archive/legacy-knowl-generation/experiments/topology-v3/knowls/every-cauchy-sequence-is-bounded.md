---
title: "Every Cauchy sequence is bounded"
description: "In any metric space, a Cauchy sequence has bounded range."
---

**Every Cauchy sequence is bounded:** Let $(X,d)$ be a metric space and let $(x_n)$ be a {{{< knowl id="cauchy-sequence" text="Cauchy sequence" >}}} in $X$. Then the set $\{x_n:n\in\mathbb{N}\}$ is a {{{< knowl id="bounded-set" text="bounded set" >}}}.

In particular, after some index all terms lie in a single {{{< knowl id="closed-ball" text="closed ball" >}}} of finite radius.
