---
title: "Path"
description: "A continuous map from the unit interval into a topological space, joining two points."
---

A **path** in a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" section="topology-core" text="topological space" >}}} $X$ is a {{{< knowl id="continuous-map" section="topology-core" text="continuous map" >}}} $\gamma:[0,1]\to X$.

The points $\gamma(0)$ and $\gamma(1)$ are called the initial and terminal points of the path. Paths are used to define {{{< knowl id="path-connected-set" text="path-connected sets" >}}} and can be viewed as a special case of a {{{< knowl id="curve" text="curve" >}}}.
