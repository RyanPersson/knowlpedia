---
title: "Convergent sequence is Cauchy"
description: "In any metric space, convergence implies the Cauchy property."
---

**Convergent sequence is Cauchy:** Let $(X,d)$ be a metric space and let $(x_n)$ be a {{{< knowl id="convergent-sequence" text="convergent sequence" >}}} with {{{< knowl id="limit-of-a-sequence" text="limit" >}}} $x\in X$. Then $(x_n)$ is a {{{< knowl id="cauchy-sequence" text="Cauchy sequence" >}}}.

This implication is one half of the definition of a {{{< knowl id="complete-metric-space" text="complete metric space" >}}}.
