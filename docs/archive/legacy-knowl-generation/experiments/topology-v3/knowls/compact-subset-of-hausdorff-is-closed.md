---
title: "Compact subset of a Hausdorff space is closed"
description: "In a Hausdorff space, every compact subset is closed"
---

**Compact subsets of Hausdorff spaces are closed:** Let $X$ be a {{{< knowl id="hausdorff-space" section="topology-separation" text="Hausdorff space" >}}} and let $K\subseteq X$ be {{{< knowl id="compact-set" text="compact" >}}}. Then $K$ is {{{< knowl id="closed-set" section="topology-core" text="closed" >}}} in $X$.

This is the separation-theoretic refinement of the metric statement {{{< knowl id="compactness-implies-closedness" text="compactness implies closedness" >}}} and is used in the {{{< knowl id="continuous-bijection-from-compact-homeomorphism-criterion" text="compact-to-Hausdorff homeomorphism criterion" >}}}.
