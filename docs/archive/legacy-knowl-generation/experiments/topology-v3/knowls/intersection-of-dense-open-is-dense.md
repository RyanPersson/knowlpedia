---
title: "Intersection of dense open sets is dense"
description: "Finite intersections of dense open sets are dense and open."
---

**Intersection of dense open sets is dense:** Let $X$ be a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" section="topology-core" text="topological space" >}}}. If $U,V\subseteq X$ are {{{< knowl id="open-set" section="topology-core" text="open" >}}} and {{{< knowl id="dense-set" section="topology-core" text="dense" >}}} in $X$, then $U\cap V$ is open and dense in $X$. More generally, the intersection of finitely many open dense subsets of $X$ is open and dense.

This elementary fact is used repeatedly in {{{< knowl id="category-argument-template" text="category arguments" >}}}; extending the conclusion from finite to countable intersections is exactly what the {{{< knowl id="baire-space" text="Baire property" >}}} provides.
