---
title: "Topology"
description: "A collection of subsets designated as open, satisfying closure axioms."
---

A **topology** on a set $X$ is a collection $\tau$ of {{{< knowl id="subset" section="shared-foundations" text="subsets" >}}} of $X$ satisfying the {{{< knowl id="topology-axioms" text="topology axioms" >}}}.

The members of $\tau$ are called {{{< knowl id="open-set" text="open sets" >}}}, and their complements are the {{{< knowl id="closed-set" text="closed sets" >}}}. A set $X$ together with a topology $\tau$ is a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" text="topological space" >}}}.
