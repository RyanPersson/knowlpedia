---
title: "Open cover"
description: "A family of open sets whose union contains a given set"
---

An **open cover** of a set $A$ in a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" section="topology-core" text="topological space" >}}} $X$ is a {{{< knowl id="cover" section="topology-core" text="cover" >}}} $\mathcal U$ of $A$ such that every $U\in\mathcal U$ is an {{{< knowl id="open-set" section="topology-core" text="open set" >}}} in $X$.

Equivalently, $\mathcal U$ is an open cover of $A$ if $A\subseteq \bigcup_{U\in\mathcal U} U$ and each $U$ is open in $X$. Open covers are the basic input in the definition of a {{{< knowl id="compact-set" text="compact set" >}}}.
