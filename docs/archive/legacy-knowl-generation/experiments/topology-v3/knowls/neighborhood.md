---
title: "Neighborhood"
description: "A set containing an open set around a point."
---

A **neighborhood** of a point $x$ in a {{{< knowl id="topological-space" text="topological space" >}}} $X$ is a subset $N\subseteq X$ such that there exists an {{{< knowl id="open-set" text="open set" >}}} $U$ with $x\in U\subseteq N$.

Neighborhoods provide a pointwise language for definitions such as {{{< knowl id="continuous-map" text="continuity" >}}} and {{{< knowl id="limit-point-accumulation-point-cluster-point" text="limit points" >}}}. In a {{{< knowl id="metric-space" section="topology-metric" text="metric space" >}}}, every {{{< knowl id="open-ball" section="topology-metric" text="open ball" >}}} centered at $x$ is a neighborhood of $x$.
