---
title: "Compact subset of a Hausdorff space is closed"
description: "In a Hausdorff space, every compact subset is closed"
---

**Compact subset of a Hausdorff space is closed:** Let $X$ be a {{< knowl id="hausdorff-space" section="topology-separation" text="Hausdorff space" >}} and let $K\subseteq X$ be {{< knowl id="compact-set" text="compact" >}}. Then $K$ is {{< knowl id="closed-set" section="topology-core" text="closed" >}} in $X$.

In particular, this implies {{< knowl id="compactness-implies-closedness" text="compactness implies closedness" >}} for compact subsets of metric spaces.
