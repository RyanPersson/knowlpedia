---
title: "Intersection of dense open sets is dense"
description: "In a Baire space, countable intersections of dense open sets are dense"
---

**Intersection of dense open sets is dense:** Let $X$ be a {{< knowl id="baire-space" text="Baire space" >}}, and let $(U_n)_{n\in\mathbb{N}}$ be a sequence of subsets of $X$ that are {{< knowl id="open-set" section="topology-core" text="open" >}} and {{< knowl id="dense-set" section="topology-core" text="dense" >}}. Then $\bigcap_{n\in\mathbb{N}} U_n$ is dense in $X$.

Such intersections are basic examples of {{< knowl id="residual-set" text="residual sets" >}} and are the typical conclusion of a {{< knowl id="category-argument-template" text="category argument" >}}.
