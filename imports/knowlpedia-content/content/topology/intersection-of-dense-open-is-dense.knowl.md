**Intersection of dense open sets is dense:** Let $X$ be a [[topology/topological-space|topological space]] and let $U,V\subseteq X$. If $U$ and $V$ are [[topology/open-set|open]] and [[topology/dense-set|dense]] in $X$, then $U\cap V$ is open and dense in $X$.

This uses that a [[topology/topology|topology]] is closed under finite [[shared-foundations/intersection|intersections]] and is a basic ingredient in many [[topology/baire-category-theorem|Baire category]] arguments (where one wants to intersect many dense open conditions).
