**Compactness implies closedness:** Let $X$ be a [[topology/hausdorff-space|Hausdorff space]] and let $K\subseteq X$ be [[topology/compact-set|compact]]. Then $K$ is [[topology/closed-set|closed]] in $X$.

In particular, this applies to subsets of any [[topology/metric-space|metric space]], since metric spaces are Hausdorff.
