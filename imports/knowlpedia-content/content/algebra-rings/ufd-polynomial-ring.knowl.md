**Gauss's theorem**: If \(R\) is a [[algebra-rings/ufd|UFD]], then the [[algebra-rings/polynomial-ring|polynomial ring]] \(R[x]\) is a UFD. More generally, \(R[x_1,\dots,x_n]\) is a UFD for all \(n\ge 1\).
