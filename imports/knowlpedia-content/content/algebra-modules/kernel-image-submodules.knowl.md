**Kernel and image are submodules**: Let $f:M\to N$ be an $R$-module homomorphism. Then $\ker(f)\le M$ and $\operatorname{im}(f)\le N$.

The kernel statement is exactly [[algebra-modules/kernels-are-submodules|kernels are submodules]], and the image statement is the analogous closure property for the [[algebra-modules/image-module|image]] of a [[algebra-modules/module-homomorphism|module homomorphism]].
