**Density of $\mathbb{Q}$:** If $a<b$ are real numbers, then there exists a rational number $q\in\mathbb{Q}$ such that $a<q<b$. In fact, there are infinitely many such rationals.

This is a fundamental approximation tool: it shows that every [[real-analysis/interval|interval]] contains “simple” numbers. Standard proofs combine the [[real-analysis/archimedean-property|Archimedean property]] with basic order properties from the [[real-analysis/order-axioms|order axioms]].
