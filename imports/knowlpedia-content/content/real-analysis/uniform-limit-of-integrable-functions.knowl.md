**Uniform limit of integrable functions:** Let $f_n:[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}$ be [[real-analysis/riemann-integrable-function|Riemann integrable]] for every $n$. If $f_n\to f$ [[real-analysis/uniform-convergence|uniformly]] on $[a,b]$, then $f$ is [[real-analysis/riemann-integrable-function|Riemann integrable]] on $[a,b]$.

With the additional conclusion of [[real-analysis/interchange-limit-integral|interchanging limit and integral]], one also gets convergence of the corresponding [[real-analysis/riemann-integral|Riemann integrals]].
