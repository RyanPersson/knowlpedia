**Subgroup of Index 2 is Normal**: Let $G$ be a [[algebra-groups/group|group]] and let $H\le G$ be a [[algebra-groups/subgroup|subgroup]]. If the [[algebra-groups/index-of-subgroup|index]] of $H$ in $G$ is $2$, then $H$ is [[algebra-groups/normal-subgroup|normal]] in $G$.

Equivalently: if there are exactly two left [[algebra-groups/coset|cosets]] of $H$ in $G$, then the left cosets equal the right cosets, so $gH=Hg$ for all $g\in G$.
