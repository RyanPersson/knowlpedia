**Proposition (Subgroups of abelian groups are normal).**
Let $G$ be an [[algebra-groups/abelian-group|abelian group]] and let $H\le G$ be a [[algebra-groups/subgroup|subgroup]]. Then $H$ is a [[algebra-groups/normal-subgroup|normal subgroup]] of $G$.

**Context.**
Normality is a conjugation-invariance condition. In an abelian group, conjugation is trivial: $ghg^{-1}=h$ for all $g,h$.
