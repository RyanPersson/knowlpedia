**Axiom of Choice:** For every indexed family $(A_i)_{i\in I}$ of nonempty sets, there exists a [[shared-foundations/function|function]] $f:I\to \bigcup_{i\in I} A_i$ such that $f(i)\in A_i$ for all $i\in I$.

Equivalently, every [[shared-foundations/indexed-family-of-sets|indexed family of sets]] of nonempty sets admits a choice function selecting one element from each set. In the presence of the other ZF axioms, this axiom is equivalent to [[shared-foundations/zorns-lemma|Zorn's lemma]] and to the [[shared-foundations/well-ordering-theorem|well-ordering theorem]].
