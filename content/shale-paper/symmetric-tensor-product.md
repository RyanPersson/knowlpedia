---
title: "Symmetric Tensor Product (·)_s"
description: "Averaging over permutations to land in the symmetric subspace"
---

For \(x_1,\dots,x_n\in H\), the **symmetric tensor**
\[
(x_1\otimes\cdots\otimes x_n)_s=\frac1{n!}\sum_{\pi\in S_n} x_{\pi(1)}\otimes\cdots\otimes x_{\pi(n)}.
\]

**Key property (paper use):**
- These generate the symmetric Fock space {{< knowl id="symmetric-fock-space" text="S(H)" >}} used for Fock–Cook quantization.

**Example:** For \(n=2\), \((x\otimes y)_s=\tfrac12(x\otimes y+y\otimes x)\).
