**Theorem.**  
Let \(q=p^m\) be a prime power. For each \(n\ge 1\), the extension
\[
\mathbb{F}_{q^n}/\mathbb{F}_q
\]
is a finite {{< knowl id="galois-extension" text="Galois extension" >}}. Its Galois group is cyclic of order \(n\), generated by the Frobenius automorphism
\[
\varphi_q:\mathbb{F}_{q^n}\to\mathbb{F}_{q^n},\qquad \varphi_q(x)=x^q,
\]
which is the \(m\)-th iterate of the {{< knowl id="frobenius-endomorphism" text="p-Frobenius" >}}.

**Examples.**
1. \(\mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{F}_{q^2}/\mathbb{F}_q)\cong C_2\), generated by \(x\mapsto x^q\).
2. \(\mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{F}_{2^6}/\mathbb{F}_{2^2})\) has order \(3\), generated by \(x\mapsto x^{4}\).
3. Subfields of \(\mathbb{F}_{q^n}\) correspond to divisors of \(n\) via {{< knowl id="galois-correspondence" text="Galois correspondence" >}}.
