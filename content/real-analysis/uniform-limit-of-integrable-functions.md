---
title: "Uniform limit of integrable functions"
description: "A uniform limit of Riemann integrable functions on a closed interval is Riemann integrable."
---

**Uniform limit of integrable functions:** Let $f_n:[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}$ be {{< knowl id="riemann-integrable-function" text="Riemann integrable" >}} for every $n$. If $f_n\to f$ {{< knowl id="uniform-convergence" text="uniformly" >}} on $[a,b]$, then $f$ is {{< knowl id="riemann-integrable-function" text="Riemann integrable" >}} on $[a,b]$.

With the additional conclusion of {{< knowl id="interchange-limit-integral" text="interchanging limit and integral" >}}, one also gets convergence of the corresponding {{< knowl id="riemann-integral" text="Riemann integrals" >}}.
