---
title: "Finitely generated torsion-free over a PID is free"
description: "Over a PID, finitely generated torsion-free modules are free."
---

**Finitely generated torsion-free over a PID is free**: Let $R$ be a PID and let $M$ be a finitely generated torsion-free $R$-module. Then $M$ is a free $R$-module.

This is the torsion-free specialization of the {{</* knowl id="structure-theorem-pid" text="structure theorem for finitely generated modules over a PID" */>}}: for a {{</* knowl id="pid" text="PID" */>}}, a {{</* knowl id="finitely-generated-module" text="finitely generated" */>}} {{</* knowl id="torsion-free-module" text="torsion-free module" */>}} has no torsion summand and must therefore be {{</* knowl id="free-module" text="free" */>}}.
