---
title: "Kernel and image are submodules"
description: "For a module homomorphism, both kernel and image are submodules."
---

**Kernel and image are submodules**: Let $f:M\to N$ be an $R$-module homomorphism. Then $\ker(f)\le M$ and $\operatorname{im}(f)\le N$.

The kernel statement is exactly {{< knowl id="kernels-are-submodules" text="kernels are submodules" >}}, and the image statement is the analogous closure property for the {{< knowl id="image-module" text="image" >}} of a {{< knowl id="module-homomorphism" text="module homomorphism" >}}.
