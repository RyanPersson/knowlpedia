---
title: "Tensor–Hom adjunction lemma"
description: "Natural isomorphism between Hom out of a tensor product and Hom into a Hom-module."
---

**Tensor–Hom adjunction lemma**: Let $R,S$ be unital rings, let ${}_S M_R$ be an $(S,R)$-bimodule, let ${}_R N$ be a left $R$-module, and let ${}_S P$ be a left $S$-module. Then there is a natural isomorphism of abelian groups
\[
\operatorname{Hom}_S(M\otimes_R N,\,P)\;\cong\;\operatorname{Hom}_R\!\bigl(N,\,\operatorname{Hom}_S(M,P)\bigr),
\]
functorial in $N$ and $P$.

This is the concrete form of the {{</* knowl id="tensor-hom-adjunction" text="Tensor–Hom adjunction" */>}} for a {{</* knowl id="bimodule" text="bimodule" */>}}, relating {{</* knowl id="tensor-product" text="tensor products" */>}} and {{</* knowl id="hom-module" text="Hom-modules" */>}}.
