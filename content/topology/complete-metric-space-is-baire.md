**Complete metric space is Baire:** Every {{< knowl id="complete-metric-space" text="complete metric space" >}} is a {{< knowl id="baire-space" text="Baire space" >}}; equivalently, in a complete metric space, every countable intersection of {{< knowl id="dense-set" text="dense" >}} {{< knowl id="open-set" text="open sets" >}} is dense.

This is the standard formulation of the {{< knowl id="baire-category-theorem" text="Baire category theorem" >}} in the language of Baire spaces, and it underlies many arguments involving {{< knowl id="meager-set" text="meager sets" >}} and {{< knowl id="nowhere-dense-set" text="nowhere dense sets" >}}.
