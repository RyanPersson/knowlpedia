---
title: "Compactness implies closedness"
description: "In a Hausdorff space, every compact set is closed"
---

**Compactness implies closedness:** Let $X$ be a {{< knowl id="hausdorff-space" text="Hausdorff space" >}} and let $K\subseteq X$ be {{< knowl id="compact-set" text="compact" >}}. Then $K$ is {{< knowl id="closed-set" text="closed" >}} in $X$.

In particular, this applies to subsets of any {{< knowl id="metric-space" text="metric space" >}}, since metric spaces are Hausdorff.
